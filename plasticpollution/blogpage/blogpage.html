New Framework for Important Marine Turtle Areas Presented at the World Conservation Congress

September 29, 2021

Every four years, the world’s largest federation of nature experts — The International Union for the Conservation of Nature (IUCN) — hosts the World Conservation Congress, an event that brings together government and non-government voices from more than 160 countries to help shape the global conservation agenda. The 2021 World Conservation Congress took place earlier this month in Marseilles, France with more than 1,400 nature institutions represented, including Oceanic Society. Speakers and participants at the event included French president Emmanuel Macron, HSH Prince Albert of Monaco, numerous ministers, CEOs, heads of international agencies, NGOs, and youth and indigenous leaders, and even Hollywood actor Harrison Ford, who delivered an impactful call to action speech at the opening ceremony...
